THE LOGO DESIGN PROCESS

The professional logo design process is described below in detail.

Briefing

I will send you a design brief that you can fill out the design brief telling me about your business, your unique position in the marketplace, and who your ideal customer is. After that, we can discuss the details of your project further

Initial Designs

After the design brief and discussing the pricing this process, I will go through the design brief you have submitted and put all my design expertise to generate some initial designs and send it to you according to the pricing

Revisions

Once you received the initial designs then we can discuss the designs and share some feedbacks until we land on the ground. If you didn't like any of the designs then we can also go through the brief once again for more.

Deliverables

In this process will generate all the required files that you will be needing to use the logo in different formats and. And I’ll always be available if you ever have questions about your logo or run into any problems with it.
